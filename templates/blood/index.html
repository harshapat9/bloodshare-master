Who is a Donor

The donor is basically a person who saves the lives of people by giving thier valuable blood to other people.The traits of a donor are:-
1.Must be within the age group of 18-60.
2.Have a blood pressure in the range of 160/90 to 110/60
3.Have a normal pulse rate between 60 to 100 per minute.
4.Hemoglobin content must be a minimum of 12gms%.
5.Should not be on any drugs such as BP or diabetes medications, hormones, aspirin, corticosteroid.
